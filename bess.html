FAQ
Why LFP chemistry?

LFP is thermally stable and safe, with long cycle life and no cobalt — well suited to stationary storage where safety and lifetime matter more than energy density.

What scale do you cover?

From behind-the-meter commercial and industrial systems through to front-of-meter grid-scale installations, built up from standard modular racks.

Indoor or outdoor?

Both — cabinet and containerised options with appropriate ingress protection and thermal management.

What about cycle life?

LFP packs typically deliver 6000+ cycles; systems are sized to your daily cycling and warranty requirements.
