FAQ
Do you supply the electricals inside?

No — we supply the PC and ABS enclosure only, engineered for solar ACDB use. The breakers, SPDs and busbars are fitted by you or your panel builder.

PC or ABS — which should I choose?

PC for the harshest outdoor exposure, high impact and clear viewing windows; ABS for a cost-effective housing in moderate conditions. Both come in UV-stabilised, flame-retardant grades.

What protection ratings are available?

Standard IP65 with IP66 options, gasket-sealed, in UL94 V-0 flame-retardant material grades.

Can enclosures be customised?

Yes — sizes, cut-outs, gland positions, windows, colour and branding to your layout.
