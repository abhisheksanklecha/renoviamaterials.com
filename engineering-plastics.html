FAQ
Do you supply virgin and recycled grades?

Yes — each family is available in virgin and post-consumer recycled (PCR) grades, with PCR content tuned to your recycled-content target.

What PCR content can you achieve?

Up to 100% depending on the polymer and application; engineering parts typically run 30–70% PCR while still meeting the mechanical specification, with property matching where needed.

Do you take our scrap for recycling?

Yes. We collect post-industrial and post-consumer engineering-plastic streams, sort them by polymer, and return compounded PCR resin — a genuine closed loop.

Can you compound to our spec?

Glass-fibre reinforcement, flame-retardant, UV-stabilised, impact-modified and coloured compounds are available across families, in virgin or PCR, to your datasheet.
