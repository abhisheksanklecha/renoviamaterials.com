FAQ
Are the chargers OCPP-compliant?

Yes — OCPP 1.6 and 2.0.1, so they integrate with major charge-point management and payment platforms.

AC or DC — what do I need?

AC wallboxes (7.4–22 kW) suit homes, workplaces and destinations where vehicles dwell; DC fast charging (30–240 kW) suits highway, fleet and public sites needing a quick top-up.

Can multiple chargers share one supply?

Yes — dynamic load management balances available capacity across chargers, avoiding a costly upgrade of the incoming supply.

Do you support installation?

We provide the hardware with specification and commissioning guidance for installers and operators.
